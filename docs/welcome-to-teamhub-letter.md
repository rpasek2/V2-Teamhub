## What You Can Do on the Web (gymteamhub.com)

The web app is where you'll find the full experience with dark mode support. As a parent, you can:

- **Dashboard** — See your linked gymnasts, upcoming events, and announcements at a glance
- **Roster** — View your gymnast's profile, medical info, emergency contacts, and floor music
- **Calendar** — Browse gym events and RSVP (going / maybe / not going)
- **Messages** — Direct message coaches and staff in real time
- **Groups** — View team posts, vote in polls, sign up for volunteer slots, and RSVP to group events
- **Competitions** — See upcoming and past meets, session times, and your gymnast's competition roster
- **Scores** — View your gymnast's scores across the season with charts and metrics
- **Skills** — Track your gymnast's skill progression by event
- **Assignments** — See practice assignments your gymnast's coach has set
- **Attendance** — Check your gymnast's attendance record
- **Progress Reports** — Read published reports from coaches about your gymnast's development
- **Marketplace** — Buy and sell used leotards, grips, and gear with other gym families
- **Resources** — Access documents and files shared by the gym
- **Announcements** — Receive important gym-wide announcements (these will pop up when you log in)

## What You Can Do on Mobile (iOS + Android) — Beta

The mobile apps are currently in beta. The mobile app is designed as a companion to the web — it's a lighter, on-the-go experience focused on quick access to your gymnast's info, messages, and notifications. For things like creating posts, managing marketplace listings, or detailed score analysis, the web app is the way to go. Mobile extras include:

- **Push notifications** — Get notified instantly when you receive a message, announcement, or update
- **Floor music offline** — Download your gymnast's floor music for playback without internet
- **Customizable bottom bar** — Choose which tabs appear in your navigation
- **Dark mode** — Full dark mode support across the entire app

## How to Get Started

1. Go to **gymteamhub.com** and create an account using your **Primary email from iClassPro**
2. Join your gym's hub using the invite code provided
3. Explore the sidebar on the left — that's how you navigate between features (Dashboard, Roster, Calendar, Messages, etc.)
4. On mobile, use the bottom navigation bar to switch between tabs. You can customize which tabs appear by going to **Settings > Customize Tabs**

## Setting Up Your Account

Once you're logged in, here are a few things to do right away:

- **Set your privacy settings** — Click **Settings** in the sidebar (gear icon at the bottom). Under **Privacy**, choose what contact info is visible to other parents
- **Update your gymnast's info** — Go to **Roster** in the sidebar, find your gymnast, and click their name. You can update details like apparel sizes, medical info, and emergency contacts from their profile
- **Send a test message** — Go to **Messages** in the sidebar. You can message coaches directly or participate in team channels
- **Check your gymnast's scores** — Go to **Scores** in the sidebar to see meet results, season bests, and qualifying score progress

## Reporting Bugs & Suggesting Features

Your feedback is critical — this is how TeamHub gets better. Here's exactly how to report something:

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon in the sidebar on web, or the Settings tab on mobile)
2. Scroll down to the **Report a Bug / Suggest a Feature** section
3. Choose either **Bug Report** or **Feature Request** using the toggle at the top
4. Fill in a **title** (short description) and **details** (what happened, what you expected, what screen you were on)
5. Click **Submit**

**What makes a good bug report:**
- What page/screen were you on?
- What did you tap or click?
- What happened vs. what you expected?
- Does it happen every time, or just once?

You can also message me directly on TeamHub if it's easier — I check messages frequently.

## What to Look For While Testing

I need your help finding anything that doesn't feel right:

1. **Does anything crash or show an error?** — Note what screen you were on and what you tapped
2. **Is any text cut off, overlapping, or hard to read?**
3. **Do your gymnasts show up correctly?** — Correct names, levels, and info
4. **Are scores and attendance accurate?** — Compare with what you know
5. **Do messages deliver?** — Send a test message and confirm the other person receives it
6. **Do notifications work?** — On mobile, check that you receive push notifications for messages
7. **Is anything confusing?** — If you can't figure out how to do something, that's a bug in my design
8. **What's missing?** — Anything you relied on in the old TeamHub that you can't find here
