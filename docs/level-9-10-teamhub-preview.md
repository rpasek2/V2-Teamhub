# Level 9/10 TeamHub Preview

Hi Level 9/10 families! I've rebuilt TeamHub from the ground up — new web app, new design, and a ton of new features. Before I roll it out to the full team, I want our Level 9 and 10 parents to test it first and help me work out any kinks.

## What's New

This is a complete rebuild, not an update to the old TeamHub. Everything has been redesigned and most features have been significantly expanded. Right now you're getting access to the **web version only** at **gymteamhub.com**. A mobile app is coming later.

## How to Sign Up

1. Go to **gymteamhub.com**
2. Click **Sign Up** and create an account
3. **You must use the email address listed as your Primary email in iClassPro.** This is how TeamHub links you to your gymnast(s). If you'd like to use a different email, message me first so I can update it before you sign up.
4. Once registered, join the hub using the invite code I'll provide
5. After joining, your gymnast(s) should automatically appear on your Dashboard

**Adding a second parent:** If you'd like another parent to have their own account, have the first parent join first, then message me on TeamHub and I'll get the second parent linked up.

**Replacing the old TeamHub:** Once you're set up on the new version, you can stop using the old TeamHub entirely. This version is fully up to date and will be the only one going forward.

## A Note on Features

Not everything will be turned on right away — I'll be enabling features gradually as testing progresses. If something in the sidebar isn't visible or accessible, that's intentional. Don't worry, it's coming!

## What You'll Find Inside

Once you're logged in, use the **sidebar on the left** to navigate between features. Here's what's available:

### Dashboard
Your home base — see your linked gymnasts, upcoming events, recent announcements, and quick links to everything else.

### Scores
This is the big one for optional families. TeamHub tracks every meet score and gives you:
- **Score history** for each event (Vault, Bars, Beam, Floor, All-Around)
- **Season bests** and **averages** updated automatically after each competition
- **Qualifying score progress** — see exactly where your gymnast stands relative to State, Regional, and National qualifying scores
- **Charts and trends** — visualize score progression across the season
- **Team placement** — see how gymnasts ranked within the team at each meet

### Competitions
- View all upcoming and past meets in one place
- Session times and rotation orders when available
- Competition rosters — see who's competing at each meet
- Location, address, and notes from coaches

### Messages
- **Direct message** coaches and staff in real time
- **Team channels** for your gymnast's group
- **Send images and files** right in the chat

### Calendar
- Full gym calendar with practices, meets, camps, and team events
- **RSVP** to events (going / maybe / not going) so coaches know who to expect

### Roster & Gymnast Profile
- View your gymnast's profile with medical info, emergency contacts, and apparel sizes
- Floor music uploaded and accessible anytime
- Skill tracking, attendance records, and coach progress reports

### Groups
- Team-specific groups for announcements, polls, and discussions
- Volunteer sign-ups for meets and events
- Group events with RSVP

## What's NOT Available Yet

- **Mobile app** — coming soon. For now, the web app works in your phone's browser, but the dedicated mobile app with push notifications isn't ready yet.
- Some features you see listed in the sidebar may be turned off or not fully populated yet — that's intentional as I roll things out.

## What I Need You to Test

Your feedback during this preview is critical. Here's what I'm specifically looking for:

1. **Can you sign up and log in without issues?** — Let me know if anything goes wrong during registration
2. **Do your gymnasts show up correctly?** — Right names, right levels, linked to the right parent
3. **Are scores accurate?** — Compare what you see in TeamHub with what you know from meets
4. **Do qualifying score trackers look right?** — Check State/Regional/National progress against your own records
5. **Can you send and receive messages?** — Try sending a message to me or a coach and confirm it goes through
6. **Is anything confusing?** — If you can't figure out how to do something, that's a problem I need to fix
7. **Is anything broken, cut off, or hard to read?** — Note what screen you were on and what you clicked
8. **What's missing?** — Anything you'd expect to see that isn't there

## How to Report Issues or Give Feedback

The easiest ways:

1. **Message me directly on TeamHub** — once you're logged in, send me a DM
2. **Use the built-in feedback tool** — go to **Settings** (gear icon at the bottom of the sidebar), scroll down to **Report a Bug / Suggest a Feature**, and submit from there. Include what page you were on, what you clicked, and what happened.

## Why You're Testing First

You already know TeamHub, so you know what to expect. Level 9 and 10 parents also tend to be the most invested in scores, qualifying progress, and competition details — exactly the features I want tested most thoroughly. Your feedback will directly shape what the rest of the team sees when the new TeamHub goes live for everyone.

Thank you for helping me get this right!
